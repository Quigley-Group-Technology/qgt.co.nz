# Privacy Policy – Āku Rawa

This Privacy Policy explains how **Quigley Group Technology Limited** (NZBN **9429051709853**), trading as **QGT** (“we”, “us”, or “our”), collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use **Āku Rawa**.

This policy is written in accordance with the [Privacy Act 2020 (New Zealand)](https://www.privacy.org.nz/privacy-act-2020/).

---

## 1. Personal information we collect

We may collect personal information from you, including information about your:

- Name
- Contact information (such as email address)
- Computer or network information (such as device information and app diagnostics)
- How you interact with Āku Rawa
- Financial and banking information that you authorise us to access via third-party services (such as bank account balances and transaction data)

Financial and banking information accessed via third-party providers is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

---

## 2. How we collect personal information

We collect personal information directly from you when you:

- Create or use the Āku Rawa application
- Authorise access to financial data via third-party services (such as banking data providers)
- Contact us for support or assistance

---

## 3. Why we collect personal information

We collect personal information in order to:

- Provide and operate the Āku Rawa application
- Enable users to securely connect to third-party services
- Allow users to view, manage, and analyse their financial information
- Provide budgeting tools and financial insights requested by the user
- Provide customer support and respond to enquiries
- Maintain, improve, and secure the application
- Meet our legal and regulatory obligations

We only collect personal information that is reasonably necessary to provide these services.

---

## 4. Providing information is optional

Providing some personal information is optional. However, if you choose not to provide certain information — including authorising access to financial and banking data — we may be unable to provide some or all features of the Āku Rawa application, including budgeting and financial insights.

---

## 5. How we keep your information safe

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information by:

- Using appropriate technical and organisational security measures
- Restricting access to personal information to what is necessary for the operation and support of the application

Where possible, financial and banking information is stored locally on your device under your control.

---

## 6. Disclosure of personal information

We do not sell personal information.

Personal information may be shared with third parties only where necessary to provide the functionality you request (for example, authorised banking data providers), or where required by law.

---

## 7. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

- Request access to the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of any personal information if you believe it is inaccurate

If you would like to request a copy of your personal information, or ask for it to be corrected, please contact us using the details below.

---

## 8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or legal requirements. The most current version will always be made available to users of Āku Rawa.
